[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I left a hundred miles of highway in my rearview
Because the only thing I want is to be near you
The moon is hanging low above the pines
And your gentle silhouette is on my mind

[Verse 1]
The dust is settling down on the open road
A million miles away from the heavy load
I left the city lights a world away
Just to see the shadows on your skin play

[Pre-Chorus]
There is a silent fire burning in the dark
Every little breath you take is leaving a mark
I pull you closer till the world fades out
You are the only thing that I care about

[Chorus]
Oh, we are finding heaven in a hayfield tonight
Wrapped up in a blanket by the pale moonlight
I want to lose myself in your sweet embrace
Ain't nothing better than this time and space

[Post-Chorus]
(Heaven tonight)
Just you and me in the fading light

[Verse 2]
Your fingertips are tracing down my sleeve
This is a moment that I never want to leave
We got a rusty radio playing soft and slow
And a kind of magic that the city will not know

[Pre-Chorus 2]
There is a silent fire burning in the dark
Every little whisper from you strikes a spark
I pull you closer till the stars blur out
You are the only dream that I care about

[Chorus]
Oh, we are finding heaven in a hayfield tonight
Wrapped up in a blanket by the pale moonlight
I want to lose myself in your sweet embrace
Ain't nothing better than this time and space

[Post-Chorus 2]
(Heaven tonight)
Just you and me in the fading light

[Bridge]
Heaven in a hayfield, falling so deep
Caught in a memory that I want to keep
The shadows are dancing, the silence is pure
You are the only answer, my beautiful cure
Just let the fire burn out of control
[Whispered] (Whisper my name... Saeed in your soul...)

[Spoken]
(There is a quiet magic when the world stands still)
(And I will stay here forever, you know I will)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, we are finding heaven in a hayfield tonight
Wrapped up in a blanket by the pale moonlight
I want to lose myself in your sweet embrace
Ain't nothing better than this time and space
